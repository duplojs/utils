Subtracts a number of days from a date.

**Supported call styles:**
- Classic: `subtractDays(input, day)` → `TheDate`
- Curried: `subtractDays(day)` → `(input) => TheDate`

`input` accepts `TheDate` or `SerializedTheDate`.
