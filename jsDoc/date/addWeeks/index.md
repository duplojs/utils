Adds a number of weeks to a date.

**Supported call styles:**
- Classic: `addWeeks(input, week)` → `TheDate`
- Curried: `addWeeks(week)` → `(input) => TheDate`

`input` accepts `TheDate` or `SerializedTheDate`.
